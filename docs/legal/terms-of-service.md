## 2. Description of the Service

Jyotish baje is a private family companion made for Nepali households. It keeps your family's Kundli (birth record) in one private place, lets you share it intentionally with the people you choose, and connects your household to Nepali religious and cultural life. The App provides:

- **My Kundli & QR (Private Kundli Sharing):** Keep your own Kundli and your family's birth profiles in a private household. When you choose to share, the App creates a QR code carrying your name and birth details; a person you trust can scan it and save your profile without retyping anything. There is no public feed, no discovery, and no sharing unless you choose to show or send your QR.
- **Household Profiles:** Add family members and keep every generation's birth records together.
- **Bikram Sambat Calendar (Patro):** A Nepali calendar with cultural and religious events, festivals, and your own household dates.
- **Religious & Cultural Guidance:** Daily context for Nepali religious life, including Temple of the Day, tithi, nakshatra, and festival information.
- **Traditional Readings:** Kundali computation, Rashifal (daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly readings), Panchanga, and related traditional calculations for the profiles you save, offered as religious and cultural guidance.
- **AI Assistant ("Jyotish Baje"):** An artificial intelligence-powered conversational assistant that answers questions grounded in the profiles and traditions you keep in the App.
- **Voice Input:** Optional voice-to-text input using your device's speech recognition.
- **Bilingual Support:** Full support for English and Nepali (Devanagari).
