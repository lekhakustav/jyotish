# Research protocol

Research exists to support a decision, not decorate a pitch. Every material external claim must
be traceable through `sources.csv` and dated because platform reach, store ratings, policies, and
creative specifications change.

## Evidence hierarchy

1. Government statistics and platform policy/specification pages.
2. Primary app-store listings and company product pages.
3. High-quality independent research with visible methodology.
4. Directional observations from public creative or sampled reviews, labeled as such.

Do not use an unsourced market-size graphic or consultant forecast as proof that the business will
succeed. App-store scale, reachable audiences, observed behavior, and our own activation/retention
data are more useful.

## Source record requirements

- Stable source ID.
- Publisher, title, URL, source type, publication/update date when known, and access date.
- The exact decision-relevant claim supported.
- Caveats such as ad reach vs active users, storefront dependence, sampled reviews, or policy
  ambiguity.
- `status=active|superseded|broken` so later research can replace stale evidence without deleting
  history.

All browsing for this repository follows root `AGENTS.md` and uses gstack `/browse`.
