# Short-form creative system

## The unit of production

The unit is a complete vertical ad, not an isolated Veo clip. The generated scene supplies a
human situation that is expensive or impossible to film; real app footage supplies credibility.

```text
8s generated source -> select/intercut 2–4s + 5–8s app proof + 2–3s payoff/CTA
```

A 12-second generated scene is allowed only when the emotional turn cannot read cleanly in eight
seconds. Longer is not automatically more persuasive. The app proposition should appear within
the first three seconds; never make viewers wait through the entire generated shot to learn what
is being advertised.

## Six initial hook families

1. **Family question:** “My mother checks everyone's rashi. I gave her one place for all of it.”
2. **Distance and belonging:** a diaspora child and parent share the same Patro/family context.
3. **Decision moment:** wedding, puja, travel, house entry, or important conversation needs timing.
4. **Explain the evidence:** replace a generic horoscope line with visible Kundali/Dasha proof.
5. **Speak, do not type:** an older user asks in Nepali and receives a readable answer.
6. **Clean alternative:** family Jyotish without consultation timers, noisy portals, or fear.

Each family is a hypothesis. Avoid turning the example copy into a permanent slogan before data.

## Visual grammar

- First frame: a face, hand, object, or action already in motion; no establishing-shot delay.
- Human scene: authentic contemporary Nepali environment, restrained color, natural performance,
  no costume-pageant exoticism.
- Transition: motivation-driven match cut or hard cut, never an effects montage hiding the app.
- App proof: one feature, legible gesture, real interface, synthetic profile.
- Text: short burned-in hook and captions added in edit, not generated by Veo.
- End: app name, one outcome-neutral CTA, and verified store destination.
- Safe zones: keep essential text/subjects out of the top 14%, bottom 35%, and outer 6% edges for
  Reels; verify every platform's current preview before publishing.

## Voice system

Test voice as a factor: warm older Nepali male, warm older Nepali female, adult child, and direct
creator voice. Every Nepali or mixed-language script needs a fluent human read. Generate clean
voice masters without music, normalize in the edit, and preserve the voice provider/model in the
media manifest.

## Anti-slop gate

Reject a render when hands, jewelry, tika, household objects, religious details, architecture, or
Nepali faces feel unstable or caricatured. Reject generic floating zodiac wheels, glowing third
eyes, cosmic portals, crystal balls, fake Sanskrit, fake phone screens, and text-like artifacts.
The cultural specificity should come from ordinary lived detail and the real app, not a pile of
symbols.

## Learning loop

Tag factor levels such as:

```text
hook=family_care
opening=human_face
speaker=adult_child
voice=older_female_ne
proof=parivar_kundali
cta=see_family_chart
language=ne
```

Attention metrics diagnose the opening. Click metrics diagnose the promise and CTA. Activation
diagnoses whether the ad found the right user and the app delivered. Retention diagnoses durable
fit. Do not “fix” an activation problem by making the hook more sensational.
