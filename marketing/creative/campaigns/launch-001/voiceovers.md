# Voiceover treatments

Each concept has three acquisition-language options. These are alternative treatments, not
three tracks to combine. A language change creates a new `creative_id`.

## Voice production contract

- **Nepali:** warm Nepali speaker, approximately 45–60 for parent-led treatments or 28–40 for
  installer-led treatments; respectful `तपाईं`; natural Nepal cadence; target 135–150 wpm.
- **English:** warm Nepali or Nepali-diaspora English, not a generic luxury-ad accent; target
  145–155 wpm.
- **Hinglish:** conversational adult voice, target 145–155 wpm. Hinglish is an ad-language
  option only. The proof footage must show English mode and the copy must never imply Hindi UI.
- Do not clone or imitate a public figure, pandit, creator, or real customer. Record the model,
  voice ID, settings, consent/rights basis, and output `media_id`.
- Ask fluent reviewers to approve Nepali and Hinglish before synthesis and again against the
  finished audio. Do not "correct" natural Nepali into Sanskrit-heavy copy.
- Aim for the spoken proposition to land by 2.5 seconds. Let the first real app proof appear
  while the voice continues. Leave 250–350 ms of air before the final CTA.
- The standard final line is **"Meet Jyotish Baje / ज्योतिष बाजे हेर्नुहोस्"** while the store
  URL is missing. Replacing it with **"Download"** after destination approval creates a new
  creative treatment.

## 01 — `fam001` / The question is about family

**Delivery:** affectionate confidence, not worry; adult-child narrator.

- **Nepali:** “ज्योतिषका प्रश्न आफ्नै मात्र हुँदैनन्। परिवारका प्रोफाइल राख्नुहोस्, सही सदस्य रोज्नुहोस्, अनि ज्योतिष बाजेलाई उसैबारे सोध्नुहोस्—नेपाली वा English मा।”
- **English:** “Astrology questions are rarely only about you. Save the family profiles you choose, select the right person, and ask Jyotish Baje about them—in Nepali or English.”
- **Hinglish:** “Jyotish ke sawaal sirf apne baare mein nahin hote. Family profiles save kijiye, sahi person choose kijiye, aur Jyotish Baje se unke baare mein poochhiye—Nepali ya English mein.”

## 02 — `nep001` / Made to be read in Nepali

**Delivery:** immediate recognition; primary-user dignity, never a patronizing child voice.

- **Nepali:** “एप बुझ्न आमाले आफ्नो भाषा छोड्नु पर्दैन। ज्योतिष बाजे नेपाली र English दुवैमा छ। भाषा बदल्नुहोस्, अनि आफ्नै स्क्रिनमा हेर्नुहोस्।”
- **English:** “Your family should not have to leave its language at the app door. Switch Jyotish Baje between Nepali and English, and see the real screen for yourself.”
- **Hinglish:** “App samajhne ke liye Maa ko apni language chhodni nahin chahiye. Jyotish Baje Nepali aur English mein hai—language switch kijiye aur real screen dekhiye.”

## 03 — `pat001` / The family date keeper

**Delivery:** practical household competence; a gentle smile in the voice.

- **Nepali:** “जन्मदिन, पूजा, परिवारका मिति—सबैको ताल नेपाली पात्रोसँग मिल्छ। विक्रम संवत् मिति र तिथि हेर्नुहोस्, अनि आफ्नो कार्यक्रम नेपाली मितिमै राख्नुहोस्।”
- **English:** “Birthdays, puja, family dates—your household keeps its own rhythm. Browse Bikram Sambat dates and tithis, then save an event in the Nepali date.”
- **Hinglish:** “Birthday, puja, family dates—ghar ka rhythm alag hota hai. Bikram Sambat date aur tithi dekhiye, phir event ko Nepali date mein save kijiye.”

## 04 — `kun001` / Built from a real Kundali

**Delivery:** measured and factual; no "ancient secret" tone.

- **Nepali:** “यो केवल राशिको सामान्य लाइन होइन। जन्म विवरणबाट कुण्डली गणना हुन्छ—लग्न, राशि, नक्षत्र र दशासहित। प्रमाण एपमै खोल्नुहोस्।”
- **English:** “This is more than a generic zodiac line. Enter birth details to calculate a Kundali, then inspect the Lagna, Rashi, Nakshatra, and Dasha in the app.”
- **Hinglish:** “Yeh sirf generic zodiac line nahin hai. Birth details se Kundali calculate kijiye, phir Lagna, Rashi, Nakshatra aur Dasha app mein dekhiye.”

## 05 — `dsh001` / Which life chapter is this?

**Delivery:** reflective orientation; never ominous or suspenseful.

- **Nepali:** “अहिले कुन जीवन-अध्याय चलिरहेको छ? आफ्नो महादशा र अन्तर्दशाको समयरेखा, अर्को महादशाको मिति, र के कुरामा ध्यान दिने—एपमै हेर्नुहोस्।”
- **English:** “Which life chapter are you in now? Explore your current Mahadasha and Antardasha boundaries, the next Mahadasha date, and the traditional guidance behind them.”
- **Hinglish:** “Abhi kaunsa life chapter chal raha hai? Current Mahadasha aur Antardasha boundaries, next Mahadasha date, aur traditional guidance ek jagah explore kijiye.”

## 06 — `muh001` / Before the family chooses a time

**Delivery:** calm planning, not urgency; household decision-maker narrator.

- **Nepali:** “घर प्रवेशजस्तो कामको समय छान्नुअघि कारण पनि बुझ्नुहोस्। मुहूर्त खोज्नुहोस्, अनि त्यस समयलाई समर्थन गर्ने पञ्चाङ्गका आधार हेर्नुहोस्।”
- **English:** “Before choosing a time for a family milestone, understand the reasons. Find a Muhurat, then see the Panchang factors behind that traditional guidance.”
- **Hinglish:** “Family milestone ka time choose karne se pehle reason bhi samajhiye. Muhurat dhoondhiye, phir us traditional timing ke Panchang factors dekhiye.”

## 07 — `mat001` / A score is not the whole relationship

**Delivery:** contemporary, equal, and lightly warm; no wedding-announcer voice.

- **Nepali:** “सम्बन्ध एउटा अंकभन्दा ठूलो हुन्छ। परम्परागत कुण्डली मिलान हेर्नुहोस्, प्रत्येक आधार किन आएको बुझ्नुहोस्—अन्तिम निर्णय तपाईंहरूकै हो।”
- **English:** “A relationship is bigger than one score. Explore traditional Kundli matching, see the evidence behind each part, and keep the real decision where it belongs—with both of you.”
- **Hinglish:** “Relationship ek score se bada hota hai. Traditional Kundli matching dekhiye, har part ka evidence samajhiye—real decision dono ka hi rahega.”

## 08 — `voc001` / Ask without typing

**Delivery:** effortless and capable; older speaker or a warm adult-child narrator.

- **Nepali:** “टाइप गर्न मन छैन? माइक थिचेर आफ्नो प्रश्न भन्नुहोस्, आएको वाक्य सच्याउनुहोस्, अनि मात्र ज्योतिष बाजेलाई पठाउनुहोस्।”
- **English:** “Do not feel like typing? Say your question, check or edit the transcript, and only then send it to Jyotish Baje.”
- **Hinglish:** “Typing ka mood nahin? Apna sawaal boliye, transcript ko check ya edit kijiye, phir Jyotish Baje ko send kijiye.”

## 09 — `dia001` / One family, two time zones

**Delivery:** intimate connection rather than homesickness or guilt.

- **Nepali:** “दूरीले परिवारको सन्दर्भ छुट्नुपर्दैन। भरोसा गर्ने व्यक्तिसँग Parivar QR बाट जन्म प्रोफाइल बाँड्नुहोस्; पाउने व्यक्तिले सम्बन्ध रोजेर मात्र राख्छ।”
- **English:** “Distance does not have to break the family context. Share a birth profile by Parivar QR with someone you trust; they choose the relationship before saving it.”
- **Hinglish:** “Distance se family context tootna zaroori nahin. Kisi trusted person ke saath Parivar QR se birth profile share kijiye; woh relationship choose karke hi save karte hain.”

## 10 — `rel001` / When the question is about your child

**Delivery:** attentive parental care without anxiety.

- **Nepali:** “प्रश्न आफ्नो छोराबारे हो भने उत्तर पनि उसकै प्रोफाइलबाट आउनुपर्छ। परिवारको सही सदस्य रोज्नुहोस्, अनि ज्योतिष बाजेलाई सोध्नुहोस्।”
- **English:** “When the question is about your child, the context should come from the profile you selected for them. Choose the family member, then ask Jyotish Baje.”
- **Hinglish:** “Sawaal aapke child ke baare mein hai, toh context bhi unke selected profile se aana chahiye. Family member choose kijiye, phir Jyotish Baje se poochhiye.”

## 11 — `pan001` / Before the day begins

**Delivery:** useful morning orientation; no prophetic cadence.

- **Nepali:** “दिन सुरु हुनुअघि आजको तिथि र समयको सन्दर्भ बुझ्नुहोस्। तिथि, नक्षत्र, योग, करण र स्थानअनुसारका पञ्चाङ्ग विवरण एपमै हेर्नुहोस्।”
- **English:** “Before the day begins, understand its traditional calendar context. See today's Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana, and location-based Panchang timings in the app.”
- **Hinglish:** “Din shuru hone se pehle aaj ka traditional calendar context samajhiye. Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana aur location-based Panchang timings app mein dekhiye.”

## 12 — `rev001` / From a birth detail to a Kundali

**Delivery:** quiet intergenerational continuity, followed by a restrained payoff.

- **Nepali:** “परिवारले सम्झेर राखेको जन्म विवरणबाट व्यक्तिगत कुण्डली सुरु हुन्छ। विवरण भर्नुहोस्, गणना हेर्नुहोस्, अनि आफ्नो राशि र नक्षत्रको वास्तविक एप नतिजा खोल्नुहोस्।”
- **English:** “A personal Kundali starts with the birth details your family remembers. Enter them, watch the calculation, and reveal the Rashi and Nakshatra in the real app.”
- **Hinglish:** “Personal Kundali family ke yaad rakhe birth details se shuru hoti hai. Details bhariye, calculation dekhiye, aur real app mein Rashi aur Nakshatra reveal kijiye.”
