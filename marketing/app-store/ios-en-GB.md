## Full description

Your Kundli is a personal religious record. Jyotish Baje gives Nepali Hindu families a private,
intentional way to keep and share the birth information behind their Kundlis.

MY KUNDLI & QR

Keep your own Kundli and the profiles you trust in one private household. When you choose to
share, Jyotish Baje creates a QR containing your name and birth details. The person you trust can
scan it, choose how they know you and save the profile without typing sensitive details again.

BUILT FOR NEPALI HINDU FAMILIES

Use Bikram Sambat dates, Nepali and English language options, Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana,
festivals and household events in an experience designed around Nepali religious life.

RELIGIOUS GUIDANCE IN ONE PLACE

Open the Religious tab for daily context, Temple of the Day, Patro and Jyotish Baje. Explore
Kundli, Dasha, Rashifal, relationship guidance, Muhurat and other traditional calculations from
the profiles you choose to save.

PRIVATE BY CHOICE

There is no public feed. QR sharing happens only when you choose to show or send your code. A QR
contains name and birth details, so share it only with people you trust. Calculated Kundli data
and relationship labels are not included in the shared QR payload.

Jyotish Baje provides traditional religious and cultural guidance. It does not guarantee future,
medical, legal or financial outcomes.
