# Advertising claims and privacy gate

This is an operating checklist, not legal advice.

## Truthful proof hierarchy

1. Show the shipped screen or deterministic result.
2. Describe the user action: calculate, compare, explore, ask, save, or receive guidance.
3. Describe traditional interpretation as interpretation, not scientific prediction.
4. State uncertainty when birth time or calculation boundaries matter.

## Allowed directions

- “See your Kundali, Dasha, Rashifal, and Nepali Patro in one place.”
- “Ask Jyotish Baje about your family using the profiles you choose to add.”
- “Explore traditional Kundli matching with the evidence explained.”
- “Find a Muhurat and understand why the time is considered supportive.”
- “Available in Nepali and English.”

Each claim still requires a current app capture proving it.

## Prohibited directions

- Guaranteed marriage, pregnancy, money, health, visa, job, or exam outcomes.
- “Your child is in danger,” death predictions, curses, doom, or countdown fear.
- Medical, legal, or financial instructions presented as astrology-backed certainty.
- Caste verdicts, reproductive judgments, or using traditional matching as a command to start
  or end a relationship.
- Fake testimonials, fabricated download counts, fake pandits, or invented app screens.
- Pressure to purchase gemstones, puja, or remedies; the app prioritizes free and low-cost
  practices.
- Claims that an AI-generated scene depicts a real customer or real event.

## Current privacy launch blocker

`docs/legal/privacy-policy.md` currently says the app does not access the camera and does not
collect usage analytics. The repository now contains Parivar QR camera access and first-party
product analytics uploaded to Supabase. Before paid acquisition, install attribution, or public
claims about privacy:

1. Reconcile the policy with actual iOS and Android behavior.
2. Review App Store and Google Play privacy disclosures.
3. Define lawful basis, retention, access, deletion, and consent where required.
4. Decide whether to use platform-native attribution, SKAN/AdServices, or an MMP.
5. Update this marketing contract after legal/product approval.

Do not add IDFA, cross-app tracking, fingerprinting, or a third-party attribution SDK as an
incidental marketing change.

## Capture privacy

Only synthetic marketing households may appear in app footage. Never show a real person's name,
birth date/time/place, family relationship, QR payload, chat content, notification, email,
account token, or analytics identifier. Keep the synthetic fixture definition versioned so the
same proof can be reproduced.
